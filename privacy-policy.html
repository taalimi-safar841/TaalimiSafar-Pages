1 About This Application

Core Purpose

The platform is built to provide guidance and counselling for underprivileged communities in smaller Indian cities. Its focus areas — education, e-skills, government schemes, business compliance, and employment opportunities — directly address the barriers these communities face in accessing opportunities.

Key Features

  • Educational support: Access to curated learning resources and counselling services.
  • Skill development: Training modules designed to improve employability.
  • Government schemes: Simplified information about welfare programs, explained in local languages.
  • Business compliance: Guidance for small entrepreneurs to meet legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Employment opportunities: Local job listings and career counselling tailored to regional needs.
  • Community forum: A moderated space for peer-to-peer knowledge sharing.
  • Interactive learning: Daily quizzes and a reward points system to encourage consistent engagement.

User Benefits

  • Access to resources: Students gain structured educational support.
  • Employability skills: Practical training for real-world job readiness.
  • Awareness of schemes: Helps families benefit from welfare programs.
  • Entrepreneurial guidance: Small business owners learn compliance essentials.
  • Career opportunities

Adeeb Care Foundation

Is a Section 8 Company registered in Delhi, legally recognized under the Companies Act of India. Established on 22 July 2022, the foundation is dedicated to social welfare and community upliftment, focusing on marginalized groups through structured programs in education, healthcare, and community development.

Community Development

  • Vocational training: Building employability through practical skill development.
  • Women empowerment: Promoting financial independence and social equality.
  • Livelihood support: Helping families sustain themselves through small-scale income opportunities.

Educational Support

  • Tutoring: Providing extra academic help to disadvantaged students.
  • Scholarships: Ensuring continuity in education through financial aid.
  • Awareness campaigns: Promoting literacy and the importance of education.

Welfare Programs

  • Government schemes assistance: Helping communities access their rightful benefits.
  • Food distribution: Providing meals to families in need.
  • Housing support: Guidance and aid for shelter and basic living conditions.

Taalimi Mela – Annual Educational Event

The Taalimi Mela is Adeeb Care Foundation’s yearly educational festival in New Delhi, designed to bring together students, families, and communities in a celebration of learning and empowerment.

Event Highlights

  • Education counselling: Career guidance and scholarship awareness sessions.
  • Skill-building workshops: Training in IT, crafts, and vocational trades.
  • Community engagement: Cultural performances, debates, and awareness skits.
  • Welfare support: Food distribution and government scheme help desks.

2 Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a safe, functional, and personalised experience on Taalimi Safar.

Users may browse educational and career-related content without creating an account. Registration is required only for interactive community features such as posting questions, replying, or participating in discussions.

2.1 Personal Information

The following personal data is collected when you register and use the application:

Data Element Purpose of Collection Mandatory?
Email Address Account creation, OTP verification, and essential communications Yes
Full Name Personalisation of your account and community profile Yes
Custom Username Identification within the community forum; displayed publicly on posts Yes
Password Account authentication and security (stored encrypted) Yes
User-Generated Content Forum questions, replies, and community contributions posted by you No (optional)

Password Security: Passwords are never stored in plain text. All passwords are processed using industry-standard cryptographic hashing algorithms before storage. We do not have access to your plain-text password at any time.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

  • Daily Quiz Scores: Your performance on in-app quizzes to display your progress, calculate reward points, and generate leaderboard data.
  • Accumulated Reward Points: Points earned through platform activities, tracked to power the in-app rewards and gamification system.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location or geolocation data
  • Device contacts, phone logs, or SMS data
  • Financial or payment information (the app is free to use)
  • Government-issued identity numbers (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Biometric data of any kind

  • Security: To detect, investigate, and prevent unauthorised access, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful governmental or judicial requests.

8 Account Deletion

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your Taalimi Safar account and all associated personal data at any time. Once a deletion request is submitted, the following process will apply:

8.1 Initiating a Deletion Request

Users may request account deletion through the in-app account settings or by submitting a written request to our Data Privacy Contact at Support@adeebcarefoundation.org. Upon submission, your account will be placed in a pending deletion state and a confirmation notification will be sent to your registered email address.

8.2 48-Hour Grace Period

Following the submission of a deletion request, a mandatory grace period of 48 hours will commence. This period is provided to allow users the opportunity to reconsider and cancel the deletion if the request was made in error. During this window, the account remains accessible but is not actively used by our systems for any new data processing beyond what is required to maintain the pending deletion state.

8.3 Cancellation of Deletion Request

If you log in to your account at any point during the 48-hour grace period, the deletion request will be automatically cancelled and your account will be fully reinstated with no data removed. You will receive a notification confirming that the deletion has been successfully cancelled.

8.4 Permanent Deletion

If no login activity is detected within the 48-hour grace period, your account will be permanently and irreversibly deleted upon expiry of that period. All personal data associated with your account — including your name, email address, username, password hash, quiz scores, and reward points — will be permanently purged from our active databases and systems. Please note that this action cannot be undone, and any data deleted in this process will not be recoverable.

8.5 Public Forum Content

Content you have previously posted in the community forum (such as questions or replies) may be anonymised rather than deleted, in order to preserve the integrity of community discussions. In such cases, your username and any personally identifying information will be removed from those posts.

9 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Password Hashing: User passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic algorithms and are never stored or transmitted in plain text.
  • Encrypted Transmission: All data transmitted between the application and our servers is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS).
  • Secure Cloud Infrastructure: Our data is hosted on DigitalOcean's infrastructure, which maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance.
  • Access Controls: Access to user data is restricted on a need-to-know basis and is subject to authentication controls.
  • OTP Authentication: Sensitive account actions are protected by One-Time Password (OTP) verification via Brevo's secure email infrastructure.

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and never share your login credentials.

10 Children's Privacy
