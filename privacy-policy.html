Welcome to Taalimi Safar. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete it.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account: username, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Complete your profile: display name, field of study, profile picture, and role (Learner / Expert / Professor / Teacher).
  • Use community features: questions and answers you post.
  • Engage with content: quiz results, course progress, daily login streaks, and earned points.
  • Submit feedback: text feedback submitted through the app.

We also collect certain information automatically when you use the app:

  • Device tokens: FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) token for push notifications.
  • Usage data: daily login timestamps for streak calculation.
  • Log data: standard server logs (IP address, request timestamps) for security monitoring.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • Deliver personalized educational content relevant to your field and role.
  • Send push notifications about replies, likes, admin updates, and app announcements.
  • Calculate and award daily login rewards and achievement points.
  • Improve the app based on aggregated usage analytics.
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3 How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With other users (public data): your username and community posts are visible to other Taalimi Safar users.
  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party services (Firebase for notifications, AWS S3 for media storage, Brevo for transactional email) solely to operate the app. They may not use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with appropriate notice.

4 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by DigitalOcean with PostgreSQL databases. We implement:

  • Encrypted passwords (never stored in plain text).
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • JWT-based authentication with token blacklisting on logout.
  • Regular security monitoring and access controls.

Media files (profile pictures) are stored in AWS S3 with private access controls.

⚠️ While we implement industry-standard security practices, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your Taalimi Safar account.

5 Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: View your profile data at any time within the app.
  • Update: Edit your profile information directly in the app settings.
  • Delete: Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data (see Section 6).
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable push notifications in your device settings or within the app.
  • Data portability: Contact us to request an export of your personal data.

6 Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your Taalimi Safar account at any time. Upon deletion:

  • Your account, email, password, profile, and personal data are permanently removed.
  • Your community posts (questions & answers) are anonymised — they remain visible but your name is replaced with "Deleted User".
  • Your earned points and login history are deleted.
  • Deletion is processed within 48 hours of your request. You may cancel within this period by re-logging into the app.

To request account deletion, use the form on our Account Deletion page or go to App Settings → Delete Account.

7 Children's Privacy

Taalimi Safar is designed for students and learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information without consent, please contact us immediately and we will delete the information.

8 Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications to your device. These notifications include:

  • Replies and likes on your community posts.
  • Admin announcements and important updates.
  • Daily login reminders (if enabled).

You can disable push notifications at any time in your device's notification settings. Your FCM device token is stored only for the purpose of delivering notifications and is deleted when you log out or delete your account.

9 Third-Party Services

We integrate the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google): Push notifications — Firebase Privacy
  • AWS S3 (Amazon): Media file storage — AWS Privacy
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Transactional email — Brevo Privacy
  • DigitalOcean: Cloud hosting — DigitalOcean Privacy

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Send an in-app notification for material changes.

Continued use of Taalimi Safar after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
